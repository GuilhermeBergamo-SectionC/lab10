When a railgun may be used

Railguns are being examined for use as anti-aircraft weapons to intercept air threats, particularly anti-ship cruise missiles, in addition to land bombardment. A railgun can also be used to launch a rocket to other planets, although we still don't know how to store or generate the necessary amount of energy to do that, but maybe in a distnant future it will be the main method so we can get to other planets.
